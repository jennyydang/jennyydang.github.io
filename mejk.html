Future Plans:

In the future, not only would we like to implement MEJK for CSULB and other colleges, but we would also like to extend this application to other parking areas (ie. Disneyland, shopping plazas, etc...).

Having a rating/comment system would also be beneficial. Having a rating & commenting system could provide feedback to the driver and passenger. It will also keep the users safe by preventing abuse of the application.

We are also considering subscription based packages (student package, community package, etc..).
